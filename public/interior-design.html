Interior Design Studio - User Guide

Welcome to our Interior Design Studio! This tool allows you to create and visualize interior spaces in both 2D and 3D.

Key Features:

  • Auto-Save: Your designs are automatically saved and will persist even if you refresh the page.
  • 2D Floorplanner: Create floor plans with precise measurements.
  • 3D Visualization: View your design in 3D from multiple angles.
  • Furniture Library: Add furniture and d√©cor items from our extensive collection.

Basic Controls:

  • Draw Walls: Click the pencil icon and click-drag to create walls.
  • Move Walls: Click the move icon and drag existing walls.
  • Delete Walls: Click the delete icon and click on walls to remove them.
  • Add Furniture: Click the + button to open the furniture library.
  • Switch Views: Toggle between 2D and 3D views using the buttons at the top right.
  • Camera Controls (3D): Click and drag to rotate view, right-click and drag to pan, scroll to zoom.

Tips:

  • Press ESC to stop drawing walls.
  • Hold Shift while drawing for straight horizontal or vertical walls.
  • Double-click on corners to adjust elevation.
  • Click on rooms to name them.
